Vision

Lakireddy Bali Reddy College Of Engineering of Technical Education Mylavaram aims to become an Institution of excellence in imparting quality Outcome Based Education that empowers the young generation with Knowledge, Skills, Research, Aptitude and Ethical values to solve Contemporary Challenging Problems

Mission

  1. Develop a platform for achieving globally acceptable level of intellectual acumen and technological competence
  2. Create an inspiring ambience that raises the motivation level for conducting quality research
  3. Provide an environment for acquiring ethical values and positive attitude

LBRCE TRUST

The Lakireddy Bali Reddy Charitable Trust sponsored the Lakireddy Bali Reddy College of Engineering which is built with a vision to serve the region for generations to come and to be among the premier institutions in the country in the sphere of technical education. In the short span of its existence the College earned a respectable position among its peers in the state for its spacious and serene environment, well designed and well ventilated buildings, fully equipped and state of the art laboratories, enthusiastic and well qualified faculty and student discipline and care
